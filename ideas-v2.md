## Selected: Idea 2 — "Aurora Glass" (Ethereal Tech)

This approach best complements the silver/metallic owl mascot with its luminous, ethereal quality. The frosted glass panels and aurora gradients create a premium, futuristic feel while keeping the design accessible and not too aggressive. The mascot's blue eyes and metallic finish will pop beautifully against the deep space backgrounds.

### Implementation Notes
- Dark theme with aurora gradient mesh backgrounds
- Frosted glass (backdrop-blur) cards throughout
- Mascot featured prominently in Hero, floating with animation
- Aurora teal + violet accent colors match mascot's blue eyes
- Outfit font for English headlines, Heebo for Hebrew
- Smooth entrance animations, parallax depth effects
